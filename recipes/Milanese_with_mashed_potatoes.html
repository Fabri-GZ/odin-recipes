Milanese With Mashed Potatoes

Description

Milanesas are without a doubt one of the favorite and most classic dishes of ArgentinaÂ´s gastronomy.
Being an international stamp from Argentina to the world, there are thousands of variants with beef,
chicken, pork, fish, vegans and millions of possible accompaniments. Unmissable during the week,
and ideal for sharing with the family today you will get to know the most traditional variant:

Meat milanese with mashed potatoes

Ingredients (4 Servings)

Milanese:

  • 500g Breadcrumbs
  • 1kg Of round steak
  • 6 Eggs
  • 250g Of grated cheese
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Oregano
  • Mustard
  • Milk

Mashed Potatoes:

  • 1kg of potatoes
  • 50cc of milk
  • 100g of butter
  • 100g of cream cheese
  • Salt
  • Nutmeg

Steps

  1. Grate the bread
  2. Tenderize the milanese
  3. In a bowl, add the eggs, plenty of grated cheese, nutmeg, oregano and mustard.
  4. Pass all the milaneses through breadcrumbs
  5. Once ready, we pass the milaneses through the preparation of eggs with cheese and again through breadcrumbs.
  6. Fry in abundant oil.
  7. For the mashed potatoes, wash the potatoes very well and boil them with their peel.
  8. While warm, remove the peel from the potatoes and mash with a potato masher, add cream cheese, butter (a lot of it, that's the secret ðŸ¤«) and if necessary, milk
  9. Stir and serve along with the milaneses.
  10. Enjoy!
