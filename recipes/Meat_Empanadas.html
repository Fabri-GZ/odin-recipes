Meat Empanadas

Description

Today is the turn of the classics, the traditional ones, the world champions……The meat empanadas!
Another Argentinian classic to enjoy with the family, easy to make and really tasty, the empanada, and
especially the meat empanada, is something that you can always rely on to calm your appetite.
In case you don´t know the tastes of the stomachs that you must fill, your safest option are the:

Meat empanadas

Ingredients (1 Dozen)

  • 12 Covers for empanadas
  • 1/2kg Minced meat
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1/2 Red bell pepper
  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 Tbsp. tomato puree
  • 1 Handful of olives
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. In a pot with hot oil add the onion and the bell pepper. Let brown for a few minutes and when
    they are half cooked (transparent onion), add the garlic and a little salt and pepper.
  2. Raise the heat and add all the minced meat at once. Move the meat so it doesn't stick.
    When the meat is sealed, add the diced tomato and the 2 tablespoons of tomato puree.
    Season with paprika and cumin, a little more salt and pepper and mix well.
    Cover (not completely, leave a small hole) and cook for half an hour, stirring little by little.
  3. Remove from the heat and let cool in the pot. Add the chopped olives and mix well.
    (In this step you can add anything else you want: hard-boiled egg, raisins, potatoes, etc.)
  4. Distribute the filling in the empanada covers, and close with a traditional repulgue.
    Before putting them in the oven you can paint them with egg, but it is optional.
  5. Put our meat empanadas on a plate and bake in a strong oven until golden brown and enjoy!
